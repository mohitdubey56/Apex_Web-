Our Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and industry-relevant computer education to every student in rural Punjab — regardless of their economic background — empowering them with skills that create real career opportunities.

Our Vision

To be Punjab's most trusted computer education institution — where every student who walks in leaves with not just a certificate, but confidence, capability, and a clear career path in the digital economy of tomorrow.

Our Story

From 2010 to Today — A Story of Transformation

In 2010, Mr. Asgar Khan noticed a critical gap — thousands of Punjab's rural youth had ambitions but lacked access to quality computer training. With his MCA degree and a burning desire to serve his community, he founded Apex Computer Institute in Ludhiana.

Starting with just a handful of computers and a small batch of eager students, the institute grew entirely through word-of-mouth — one successful graduate recommending it to the next. Fourteen years and 1,200+ students later, Apex stands as a testament to what dedication and community trust can achieve.

Meet Mr. Asgar Khan

AK

Mr. Asgar Khan

Founder & Head Instructor — Apex Computer Institute
MCA — Master of Computer Applications Certified IT Trainer 14+ Years Experience Ludhiana, Punjab

Mr. Asgar Khan has dedicated 14+ years to empowering students in rural Punjab with practical computer education. His philosophy is simple: every student, regardless of background, deserves access to skills that can change their life.

With an MCA degree and professional certifications in IT training, he brings both academic depth and real-world practicality to every class. His teaching style is known for being patient, practical, and exam-focused — helping students not just understand concepts but apply them professionally.

Under his guidance, students have gone on to secure government jobs, work at CA firms, start their own businesses, and build stable careers across Punjab and beyond. His proudest achievement is not any award — it is the 1200+ lives that have been transformed.
